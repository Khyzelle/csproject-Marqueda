The Art of Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is an animation studio located in Japan that has been making animated feature films since 1985.
Their films have had a significant impact on audiences all around the world. With a mix of well-paced storytelling
and their classic soft art style and color palette, Studio Ghibli manages to romaticize the world with pastel colored glasses and child-like worldviews. However, no matter how soft their films may be,
they are an enjoyable watch for all ages.
